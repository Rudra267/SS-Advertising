Transit & Mobile Advertising

"On The Move, On Their Mind."

Transit advertising takes your brand to where people live, work, shop, and travel. At SS Advertising, we blend mobility, visibility, and creative execution across city streets.

1. LED Vans

“Moving Screens. Maximum Scenes.”

  • High-res LED screens (P6 / P4 / P3.9)
  • Sound system, GPS tracking, night visibility
  • Great for political campaigns, launches, festivals

2. Mobile Vans (Flex Branding)

“Flex Power on Four Wheels.”

  • Branded static panels with optional miking
  • Great for schools, real estate, FMCG & events
  • Highly flexible and budget-friendly

3. Auto Tops

“Capture the City’s Most Loyal Transport Mode.”

  • Durable rooftop boards with 30–90 day cycles
  • City-wide or locality-based campaigns
  • Perfect for apps, D2C, education & elections

4. Auto Stickers

“Inside Ride, Outside Reach.”

  • Rear glass, roof, partition & door branding
  • Targets daily commuters at affordable rates
  • Ideal for food, coaching, awareness drives

5. Car/Bike/Van Branding

“Personal Vehicles as Professional Billboards.”

  • Full wraps, partial wraps, magnetic stickers
  • Suited for fleets, delivery, logistics & influencers
  • Scalable for pan-city brand reinforcement

💼 Why SS Advertising for Transit Campaigns?

  • ✔ GPS-tracked units for live accountability
  • ✔ RTO permissions and legal compliance
  • ✔ High-quality printing, lamination & placement
  • ✔ Add-on street promotions available
