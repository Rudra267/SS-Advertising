Outdoor Advertising Services

"Your Brand, Our Streets – Seen. Remembered. Admired."

SS Advertising brings innovation and precision to traditional outdoor media formats, ensuring your campaigns are impactful and result-driven.

1. Hoardings / Billboards

“Own the Skyline. Dominate the Eyespace.”

  • Types: Static, LED, Sky Signs
  • Use Cases: Launches, Politics, Real Estate
  • Benefits: Day-night visibility, Local targeting

2. Center Median Boards

“Strategically Positioned. Repetitively Viewed.”

  • Perfect for FMCG, Apps, Education
  • Cost-effective with repeated visibility

3. Traffic Booth Branding

“Stationary Spots with High-Stoppage Exposure.”

  • Engages drivers & pedestrians at signals
  • Authority-approved, consistent visibility

4. Traffic Barricade Branding

“Mobile Mediums. Maximum Movement. Powerful Impact.”

  • Full-face ads with reflective graphics
  • Ideal for safety, events, announcements

5. Bus Shelter Branding

“Where Waiting Turns Into Watching.”

  • LED screens, QR codes, coupon distribution
  • Placed in business & high-traffic areas

6. No Parking Boards

“Small Signs. Massive Local Recall.”

  • Permanent or event-based visibility
  • Popular among local brands & campaigns

7. Tricycle Branding

“Eco-Advertising that Goes Places!”

  • Tri-sided boards with optional speakers
  • Ideal for door-to-door and festive promos

8. Look Walkers

“Moving Billboards with a Human Touch.”

  • Promoters carry lightweight LED boards
  • Perfect for flash sales, apps & giveaways

9. Bus Advertising

“Let Your Message Ride the City.”

  • Full wraps, side/back panels, window decals
  • Great for B2C, FMCG, travel & awareness
