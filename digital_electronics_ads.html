Digital & Electronic Media

"From Screens to Streets — Digital Impact Delivered."

In an age of attention economy, we bring your brand alive on digital displays, airwaves, walls, and screens — combining tech and creativity to drive meaningful engagement.

1. Residential Digital Screens

“Hyperlocal, High-Frequency, Home-Proximity Advertising.”

  • Screens in lifts, lobbies, clubhouses, parking zones
  • Supports images, videos, geo-tagging, dayparting
  • Ideal for clinics, schools, apps, events, festivals

2. LED Screens (Indoor & Outdoor)

“Big, Bright, Bold — 24x7 Brand Broadcasting.”

  • Mobile vans, hoardings, stage backdrops, kiosks
  • Perfect for concerts, launches, rallies, screenings
  • Features: Social tickers, audio sync, live feeds

3. Theater Advertising

“Captive Audience. Crystal-Clear Impact.”

  • Ads in PVR, INOX, Cinepolis, & local halls
  • Slides, video ads, lobby branding, ticket counters
  • High emotional engagement & guaranteed attention

4. Radio and TV Ads

“Speak to the Masses. Echo in Their Minds.”

  • FM (Red FM, Big FM), TV (GEC, news, kids)
  • Scriptwriting, jingles, interviews, L-band, scrolls
  • Local & national targeting with creative production

5. Digital Wall Painting

“Old-School Canvas with a Tech-Twist.”

  • LED-backlit murals, 3D effects, smart lighting
  • Locations: flyovers, boundaries, temples, schools
  • Ideal for CSR, health, government, and rural impact
