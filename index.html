Our Work

We reach rural and urban India through custom campaigns, cultural events, CSR initiatives, and business outreach.

Rural Activities

Reach Out! Our mobile van promotions connect your brand to the heart of rural India. Activities at Mandis, Haats, Melas, and trade days ensure massive visibility and engagement.

RWA Activities

Festivals in residential colonies and apartments help bring your message directly to the doorstep. We ensure brand visibility across urban neighborhoods.

CSR Activities

We run socially responsible events like Nellore 5K Run (Go Green) and Godavari 5K Run (Stop Pollution). These create awareness and brand value.

Large Scale Events

We’ve led major campaigns like Pushkaralu, COP-11, Save Energy drives, and more. With a decade of experience, we engage the masses at scale.

Agri Business

We partner with farming communities across South India to promote agri-products. Our campaigns uplift agricultural brands with trusted rural outreach.
